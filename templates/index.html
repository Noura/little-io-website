Little LFO

Little LFO is a semi-modular Rack Extension for Reason.

Uses

  • a monophonic oscillator section for a modular synth
  • a modulation source
  • a noise maker

Features

  • 4 oscillators
  • Enveloped and non-enveloped CV outputs (bipolar and unipolar)
  • Enveloped and non-enveloped audio outputs
  • Gate, note, and envelope CV out

Uniqueness

  • All active oscillators...

So far it's just this one plugin, but more are on the way. Email comments, questions, and suggestions to littleioaudio@gmail.com.
